# Vita – Personal Life Reflection & Stability App

Vita is a cross-platform app designed for fresh graduates and early-career professionals to build awareness, reflection, and stability in daily life. It helps track spending, energy, habits, goals, and important personal documents — all without pressure or complexity.

## Features

- **Money Awareness** – Track daily expenses and subscriptions with insights on patterns.
- **Energy & Overload** – Quick daily check-ins and weekly overload reports.
- **Gentle Day Structure** – Focus on 3 important tasks and 1 personal activity per day.
- **Life & Career Goals** – Track short- and long-term goals with realistic feasibility analysis.
- **Stability & Self-care** – Habit tracking with a personal stability score.
- **Life Documents Vault** – Secure local storage for important documents.

## Tech Stack

- Cross-platform framework: Flutter / React Native (choose your implementation)  
- Local database: SQLite / Hive  
- Custom rule-based AI for insights  
- Offline-first architecture
